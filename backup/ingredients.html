Instructions: Defrost Cookies & Dawgs in their original box. Refreeze once if desired. Store in a cool place off the floor. If Bulk, keep bag tied tightly. If you will not be serving the cookies in 3-5 days, we recommend keeping the cookies refrigerated or frozen. For longer shelf life, freeze up to 6 months.
